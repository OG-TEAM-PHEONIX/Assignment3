Group Processes
We were able to accomplish the task that was necessary for the Assignment 2, however due to the lack of communication we were running behind on our tasks that we planned to do for certain days. Even without the communication we were also lacking motivation from some team members to complete the required duties to be on schedule and make this assignment as smooth as possible. We were able to reflect on it and realise that team members need to make those changes and communicate effectively by having a set timeline and delegate those tasks, we also needed to have a strong leadership who will be able keep an eye on those tasks on regular basis so that we can ensure that no one gets left behind and feels included as part of the team. The changes are to make sure the tasks that are assigned to each team member needs to be put on Trello, the app is designed to effectively work around the schedule and assign each member a certain task that needs to be completed and can be seen my all the members. If at any point a team member feels that we are running behind on certain tasks then they can address this via Team Meetings, Email, and Microsoft teams to and address any concerns.
