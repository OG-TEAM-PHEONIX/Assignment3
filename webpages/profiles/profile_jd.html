JD Eru

S3970972 |s3970972@student.rmit.edu.au

JD Eru currently lives on Wajuk Country, otherwise known as Perth WA. JD was born in Taranaki Aotearoa and moved to Australia in 1999. JD has worked in the banking sector for the past 6 years and prior to that worked in travel. JD’s interest in IT has increased over the last few years, a natural curiosity and need to understand “How things work” has driven JD to pursue a career in IT through Open Universities Australia and RMIT, JD is currently a member of Team Phoenix. JD has not yet decided what she wants to pursue in the world of IT but is particularly interested in data science. Outside of work and studying JD is passionate about cooking, baking, and spending time with her toddlers.

Career Plan

JD is currently studying a bachelor of IT through OUA in a part time capacity. In 2024 JD plans to increase her studies to full time and begin an IT development plan at her current employer. The IT development plan is available to employees who meet certain criteria regarding length of service and diversity requirements. Completing this development plan in conjunction with her university studies will directly improve JD’s chances at being accepted in The Groups Graduate program which JD plans to apply for at the end of 2024. JD then plans to complete a graduate program within the financial services industry which includes a rotation throughout different departments. Depending on the workload JD will aim to complete her master's in data science. If this is not possible JD will apply for long service leave from her current employer to complete her masters and upon completion JD plans to apply for a job with ASIC.
