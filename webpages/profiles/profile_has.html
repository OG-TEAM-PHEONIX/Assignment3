Hasnain Syed

S3980388 |s3980388@student.rmit.edu.au

Hasnain is a student at RMIT University, representing Team Phoenix, Hasnain lives in the outer western suburbs of Melbourne. Hasnain comes from an Indian background, and is fluent in English, Urdu, and Hindi. Hasnain loves playing cricket, going for hikes outdoors and gaming. Hasnain's main IT interests are Data Science, Data Analytics and Cyber Security. Hasnain is eager to learn as much as he can about how technology works and is looking forward to expanding his knowledge and experience with Relational Databases, Python and Web Development using HTML and CSS.
