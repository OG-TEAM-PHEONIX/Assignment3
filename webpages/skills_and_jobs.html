Skills and Jobs

When presented with the opportunity to hire 4 people for the growth of PP it was important to take into consideration the current skillset of Team Phoenix and the developmental needs of the project. This involved referring to the project timeline, the plans and progress and the scope. The 4 jobs to be advertised are:

  • Cloud Engineer
  • Database developer
  • Full stack developer
  • Software engineer - Android
The fulfillment of each of these roles would result in a minimum viable product being reached in an expedited timeframe. To establish what skills would be required various job roles were researched with the most common roles and responsibilities collated and personalised to suit the needs of Parents Pantry. Given the similarity between Parents Pantry and the previously mentioned Diffords cocktail an initial search was done for jobs at Diffords, this unfortunately returned no results so mobile app employment was then researched.

Job title: Cloud Engineer

Position Purpose
No two days will be the same, as a small startup you will be responsible for the uplift and development of our new mobile app Parents Pantry. You will work closely with the database developer, cloud engineer and full stack developer. Strong communication and teamwork skills are vital.

Responsibilities

  • Ability to think outside of the box to problem solve
  • Understanding of project development process and willingness to contribute
  • Work with key stakeholders to collaborate, present and create industry leading practices
  • End to end development and testing
  • Maintain cloud solutions
  • Planning and designing cloud computing skills
Requirements
  • Experience with advanced HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, Python, MySQL and Oracle
  • Previous experience implementing cloud infrastructure for mobile apps
  • Strong written and interpersonal communication skills
  • Demonstrated experience working in a team
  • Troubleshooting and strong analytical skills
